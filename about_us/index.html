A One-Person Adventure (For Now)

#

Cocolabs is currently a pet project led by just one person—me! 🚀 This initiative is designed with the vision to grow and evolve over time. It’s an open-source project aimed at supporting Mac admins by providing better tools 🛠️ and raw information 📊 to make their jobs easier and more efficient. While it’s a solo endeavor for now, there’s plenty of room for expansion, collaboration 🤝, and community involvement in the future.
